Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase. If something isn't right, we're here to help.

Our 30-Day Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items in new, unused condition with original packaging. Some exclusions apply.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log in to your account and go to My Orders.
  2. Select the order containing the item you wish to return.
  3. Click "Return Items" and follow the instructions.
  4. Print the prepaid return shipping label (for domestic returns).
  5. Pack the item securely in its original packaging, attach the label, and drop off at any carrier location.

If you don't have an account, you can contact our support team with your order number and we'll assist you.

Refunds

Once we receive your return, we'll inspect the item and notify you of the status. If approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email.

Exchanges

To exchange an item for a different size or color, please initiate a return and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures the fastest processing.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Gift cards
  • Final sale items (marked as such on product page)
  • Personal care items that have been opened or used (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items returned after 30 days

Return Shipping Costs

For domestic returns, we provide a prepaid shipping label. The cost ($5.99) will be deducted from your refund unless the return is due to our error (e.g., wrong item shipped, defective product). For international returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

Questions?

If you have any questions about returns, please contact our support team.
