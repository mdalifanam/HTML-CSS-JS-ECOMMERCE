Shipping Information

Learn about our shipping options, rates, and delivery times.

Domestic Shipping (US)

We offer two shipping options within the United States:

  • Standard Shipping: $5.99 (3-5 business days). Free on orders over $50.
  • Express Shipping: $12.99 (1-2 business days).

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight. Delivery typically takes 7-14 business days, but may vary due to customs.

Please note: Customs duties, taxes, and fees may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). You'll receive a confirmation email once your order ships, with tracking information.

Shipping Carriers

We primarily use USPS, UPS, and FedEx for domestic shipments, and DHL or local carriers for international deliveries.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order from your My Orders page after logging in.

Shipping to PO Boxes

We can ship to PO boxes via USPS. Please note that express shipping may not be available for PO box addresses.

Questions?

If you have any questions about shipping, please contact our support team.
